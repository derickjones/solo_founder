# Gospel Study App - Promo Video Narration Script

## Target Audience
LDS Facebook groups and community members looking for enhanced scripture study tools.

## Video Length
60-75 seconds when read at normal pace

---

## Script

### Opening - Warm, inviting tone
"Struggling to find deeper meaning in your daily scripture study? Wish you had a personal gospel study companion that could answer your questions and guide your learning?"

### Problem identification - Relatable tone
"We've all been there. Reading the scriptures, but feeling like we're missing something. Wondering about context, symbolism, or how ancient teachings apply to our modern lives."

### Solution introduction - Excited, confident tone
"Introducing the Gospel Study App - your AI-powered scripture study companion that transforms the way you engage with the gospel."

### Feature demonstration - Clear, informative tone
"Ask any question about the scriptures and get instant, thoughtful answers. Generate personalized study guides for any topic. Create custom lesson plans for family home evening or Sunday School."

### Visual Guides highlight - Enthusiastic tone
"Plus, our brand-new Visual Guides feature creates beautiful, shareable infographics that bring gospel principles to life. Perfect for social media, presentations, or personal study."

### Come Follow Me integration - Warm, community-focused tone
"Seamlessly integrates with Come Follow Me curriculum, providing weekly insights, daily thoughts, and podcast-style content that fits your busy schedule."

### Testimonial/Community - Authentic tone
"Join thousands of members who have already transformed their scripture study. From busy parents to seminary teachers, everyone's finding new depth in the gospel."

### Call to action - Encouraging, clear tone
"Ready to revolutionize your scripture study? Download the Gospel Study App today. Free to try, premium features available. Your spiritual growth journey starts now."

### Closing - Warm, inviting
"The Gospel Study App - Where ancient wisdom meets modern technology."

---

## Production Notes

### Timing
- Total script: ~60-75 seconds when read at normal pace
- Perfect for social media video length
- Each paragraph represents about 7-10 seconds of narration
- Pause briefly between sections for visual transitions

### Delivery Tips
- Use a warm, conversational tone throughout
- Emphasize key phrases like "AI-powered," "Visual Guides," "Come Follow Me"
- Speak clearly and at a moderate pace
- Add natural enthusiasm when highlighting features
- End with confidence and warmth

### Key Features to Highlight Visually
1. Q&A functionality (ask questions, get answers)
2. Visual Guides feature (show infographic creation)
3. Study guides generation
4. Come Follow Me integration
5. Lesson plan creation
6. Premium subscription at $3.99/month

### Target Platform Specs
- Facebook video: Square (1:1) or vertical (4:5) format preferred
- Keep text overlays large and readable on mobile
- Include captions for accessibility
- Use engaging thumbnails

This script is designed to resonate with LDS audiences while highlighting your app's unique features and benefits!
