# NotebookLM Imagery Instructions for Gospel Study App

## Context
This document provides instructions for generating appropriate Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints (LDS) themed imagery for the Gospel Study App marketing materials using NotebookLM's image generation capabilities.

## App Overview
The Gospel Study App is an AI-powered scripture study companion that helps LDS members:
- Ask questions about scriptures and receive thoughtful answers
- Generate personalized study guides for any gospel topic
- Create custom lesson plans for family home evening or Sunday School
- Access Visual Guides that create beautiful, shareable infographics
- Integrate seamlessly with Come Follow Me curriculum
- Generate weekly insights, daily thoughts, and podcast-style content

## Visual Style Guidelines

### Core Visual Elements
- **Clean, modern design** with reverent aesthetic
- **Warm, inviting colors** - soft blues, golds, warm whites, earth tones
- **Professional yet approachable** tone
- **Family-friendly** imagery that appeals to all ages
- **Technology integration** showing modern tools enhancing spiritual study

### Appropriate LDS Imagery Themes

#### Scripture Study Scenes
- Families gathered around scriptures with tablets/phones nearby
- Individuals studying with both physical scriptures and digital devices
- Study groups with mixed traditional and digital resources
- Parents teaching children with interactive digital aids

#### Religious Symbols & Elements
- Open scriptures (Book of Mormon, Bible, D&C, Pearl of Great Price)
- Temple silhouettes or architectural elements
- Peaceful landscapes (mountains, gardens, serene settings)
- Light and illumination themes
- Trees (especially olive trees referencing scripture)
- Doves, lambs, and other scriptural symbols

#### Modern Technology Integration
- Smartphones and tablets displaying scripture study apps
- Families using devices together for gospel learning
- Clean, modern interfaces showing the app in use
- Visual guides and infographics being created or shared
- People engaging with digital study materials

#### Community & Learning
- Sunday School classes with mixed traditional/digital learning
- Seminary students using modern study tools
- Missionaries with updated teaching methods
- Multi-generational families studying together
- Home evening scenes with interactive elements

### Color Palette Recommendations
- **Primary**: Soft navy blue (#2C3E50), warm gold (#F39C12)
- **Secondary**: Gentle teal (#16A085), cream (#FFF8DC)
- **Accent**: Soft coral (#E67E22), sage green (#95A5A6)
- **Neutral**: Warm grays, off-whites, natural wood tones

### What to Avoid
- Dark or intimidating imagery
- Overly formal or institutional settings
- Imagery that might appear sectarian to other Christian denominations
- Photos that look outdated or disconnected from modern life
- Cluttered or busy compositions

## Sample Prompts for NotebookLM

### For App Screenshots/Mockups
"Create a clean, modern mobile app interface showing a scripture study app. The screen displays a question about Book of Mormon verses with an AI-generated answer below. Use warm, inviting colors like soft navy and gold. The interface should look professional yet approachable, with clear typography and intuitive navigation elements."

### For Family Study Scenes
"Generate an image of a diverse LDS family (parents and children aged 8-16) gathered in a comfortable living room for evening scripture study. They have both physical scriptures and a tablet displaying the Gospel Study App. The lighting is warm and inviting, with soft natural light. The scene conveys peace, learning, and togetherness. Use warm color palette with blues and golds."

### For Individual Study
"Create an image of a young adult studying scriptures at a clean, modern desk. They have an open Book of Mormon next to a laptop displaying colorful study guides and visual infographics. The background shows soft natural light through a window. The atmosphere is peaceful and focused, conveying personal spiritual growth and modern learning."

### For Visual Guides Feature
"Generate an elegant, shareable infographic about a gospel principle (like faith or charity). Use clean typography, scriptural imagery like olive branches or doves, and a warm color scheme of navy blue and gold. The design should be suitable for social media sharing and look professionally created but approachable."

### For Come Follow Me Integration
"Create an image showing the Come Follow Me manual alongside a smartphone displaying the Gospel Study App. Include visual elements like study guides, daily thoughts, and podcast icons on the phone screen. The setting is a peaceful home study area with warm lighting and modern, clean aesthetics."

## Technical Specifications

### For Social Media
- **Instagram/Facebook**: 1080x1080 (square) or 1080x1350 (vertical)
- **Facebook Cover**: 1200x630
- **Twitter**: 1200x675
- Ensure text is readable at small sizes
- High contrast for mobile viewing

### For Marketing Materials
- **Web headers**: 1920x800
- **App store screenshots**: Device-specific dimensions
- **Print materials**: 300 DPI minimum
- Vector-style elements when possible for scalability

## Brand Voice in Imagery
The visual style should reflect the app's personality:
- **Approachable**: Not intimidating or overly academic
- **Modern**: Current with technology trends but timeless in spiritual appeal
- **Inclusive**: Welcoming to all LDS members regardless of age or background
- **Reverent**: Respectful of sacred themes and scriptures
- **Empowering**: Shows how technology enhances rather than replaces spiritual study
